# CS50 Certificate

If you submit and receive a score of at least 70% on each of this course’s problems as well as its final project, you will be eligible for a free [CS50 Certificate](https://cs50.harvard.edu/certificates) like the below. For a [verified certificate](https://www.edx.org/verified-certificate) from edX, register at [cs50.edx.org/python](http://cs50.edx.org/python). Email [certificates@cs50.harvard.edu](mailto:certificates@cs50.harvard.edu) with any questions.
